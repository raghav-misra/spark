Why Spark?

Handwritten quizes and assignments are boring, why do digital ones have to be too? Spark aims to make creating digital learning experiences painless for both students and teachers.

Spark is a platform that allows you to create and take assignments. You can create and customize interactive text-based games/RPGs through out editor. The editor can be used to customize the layout of your game based on whatever you are teaching. For an example on how to do this, play the demo.
